Hamiltonian Cycles and the Petersen Graph

This is an unpublished survey paper of Hamiltonian cycles and the Petersen graph. Included is a rigorous proof that the Petersen graph is the unique smallest hypohamiltonian graph. The paper was written as part of a capstone project for a bachelors in mathematics. Undertaking of this project required a self guided study of graph theory.     link

About Me

Currently, I'm a Research Analyst and aspiring statistician in the Division of Clinical Pharmacology in the Pediatrics department of the University of Utah. Members of the division come to me when they need a fast and efficient analysis of their data. I have a graduate level certificate in big data and expertise in the R programming language among others. Additionally, I am able to generate professional documents (LaTeX) and create informative and aesthetic data visualizations. These skills combined make me an asset for the researchers I work with.

I am most interested in the creative aspects of the data analysis process and I enjoy the challenge these types of problems provide. When I am not solving problems, I enjoy cooking food for friends and building furniture or tools. On occasion I will venture to the more wild places of Utah and beyond.
